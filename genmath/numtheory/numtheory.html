Number Theory

The goal of this project is to lay out a series of small bite-sized lessions roughly following what would be covered in an undergraduate course on number theory. The primary text I'll be following is Elementary Number Theory, 2nd edition by Underwood Dudley . The goal is to have about two (or perhaps three) items per chapter in the corresponding text, building up to a key result at the end of each.
