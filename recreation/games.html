Here I'm hoping to add links to some of my favorite games to be found online.

Orisinal

Old-school Flash games dating back to 2000, courtesy of developer Ferry Halim. Like your games low-stakes and relaxing? Enjoy popping bubbles and catching falling stars? This retro game collection is for you.

EyezMaze

Old-school Flash and HTML5 games leaning more in the puzzle direction. Do you enjoy surrealism? Exploring weird interactions of game mechanics? This coolection from developer On Nakayama might be your thing.

JayIsGames

Technically not a game site on its own, but a review site for casual online games and mobile games. Absolutely worth scrolling through their archives for some classic games.
